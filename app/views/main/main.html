How it Works - Free Game Servers

You offer free game servers? What’s The Catch?

At FreebieServers.com, we provide our clients with free game servers as well as an exclusive VIP server package.  Our free package is 100% free to use and will allow you to your server however you want.  The only string attached is that your server will be fitted with an MOTD that displays an ad from our ad network. Location selection is also restricted with the free package.  Our VIP package is like our Free, however YOU choose the MOTD and you can have as many slots as you see fit. All for one flat price! You are provided with one free server per IP address and community – please don’t be greedy the servers are for everyone

Tiered Server Support

While we provide our servers without any money needed, our support structure is tiered based on paying vs non-paying clients.  While we provide support for our free servers, priority is given to tickets submitted by our VIP members.  The response time for your support ticket may will vary, but are structured as follows:

  • Free Members:Ticket response within first 24-36 hours
  • VIP Members:Ticket response within the first 12 hours

Please note that free customer support will be limited to preventing downtime and crashes, mod installs and configurations are your responsibility.
